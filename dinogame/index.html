Dino Run!

Re-imagining of the classic Chrome game

This project, started as an final class project, explored the idea of what happens to the Chrome Offline Dino after the internet comes back on

The player controls the dino using the keyboard and tries to survive as long as possible until the Dino runs out of time. When the timer is reached, our beloved dino is taken out by the "internet asteroid" and goes extinct, waiting until the internet comes back on.

The first and current iteration of this game uses p5.js, a Processing port made by the ITP department in NYU. The sprites were recreated by me, credits to the original designs for the dino by the Google Chrome team.
